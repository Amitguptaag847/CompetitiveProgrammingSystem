<p style="margin-bottom: 0cm; border: none; padding: 0cm; font-variant: normal; letter-spacing: normal; font-style: normal; font-weight: normal; orphans: 2; widows: 2">
<span style="display: inline-block; border: none; padding: 0cm"><font face="Arial, sans-serif"><font size="2" style="font-size: 10pt"><font color="#222222"><i>Unlucky</i>&nbsp;year
in Aryanagar is such a year that its number&nbsp;<font size="3" style="font-size: 12pt"><i>n</i></font>&nbsp;can
be represented as&nbsp;<font size="3" style="font-size: 12pt"><i>n</i></font> <font size="3" style="font-size: 12pt">= </font><font size="3" style="font-size: 12pt"><i>x</i></font><sup><font size="2" style="font-size: 9pt"><i>a</i></font></sup><sup> </sup><font size="3" style="font-size: 12pt">+ </font><font size="3" style="font-size: 12pt"><i>y</i></font><sup><font size="2" style="font-size: 9pt"><i>b</i></font></sup>,
where&nbsp;<font size="3" style="font-size: 12pt"><i>a</i></font>&nbsp;and&nbsp;<font size="3" style="font-size: 12pt"><i>b</i></font>&nbsp;are
non-negative integer numbers.</span></font></font></font></p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 0cm; border: none; padding: 0cm; font-variant: normal; letter-spacing: normal; font-style: normal; font-weight: normal; orphans: 2; widows: 2">
<span style="display: inline-block; border: none; padding: 0cm"><font face="Arial, sans-serif"><font size="2" style="font-size: 10pt"><font color="#222222">For
example, if&nbsp;<font size="3" style="font-size: 12pt"><i>x</i></font> <font size="3" style="font-size: 12pt">= 2</font>&nbsp;and&nbsp;<font size="3" style="font-size: 12pt"><i>y</i></font> <font size="3" style="font-size: 12pt">= 3</font>&nbsp;then
the years&nbsp;<font size="2" style="font-size: 11pt">4</font>&nbsp;and&nbsp;<font size="2" style="font-size: 11pt">17</font>&nbsp;are&nbsp;<i>unlucky</i>&nbsp;(<font size="3" style="font-size: 12pt">4 = 2</font><sup><font size="2" style="font-size: 9pt">0</font></sup> <font size="3" style="font-size: 12pt">+ 3</font><sup><font size="2" style="font-size: 9pt">1</font></sup>,&nbsp;<font size="3" style="font-size: 12pt">17 = 2</font><sup><font size="2" style="font-size: 9pt">3</font></sup> <font size="3" style="font-size: 12pt">+ 3</font><sup><font size="2" style="font-size: 9pt">2</font></sup> <font size="3" style="font-size: 12pt">= 2</font><sup><font size="2" style="font-size: 9pt">4</font></sup> <font size="3" style="font-size: 12pt">+ 3</font><sup><font size="2" style="font-size: 9pt">0</font></sup>)
and year&nbsp;<font size="2" style="font-size: 11pt">18</font>&nbsp;isn't&nbsp;<i>unlucky</i>&nbsp;as
there is no such representation for it.</span></font></font></font></p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 0cm; border: none; padding: 0cm; font-variant: normal; letter-spacing: normal; font-style: normal; font-weight: normal; orphans: 2; widows: 2">
<span style="display: inline-block; border: none; padding: 0cm"><font face="Arial, sans-serif"><font size="2" style="font-size: 10pt"><font color="#222222">Such
interval of years that there are no&nbsp;<i>unlucky</i>&nbsp;years in
it is called&nbsp;<i>The Golden Age</i>.</span></font></font></font></p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 0cm; border: none; padding: 0cm; font-variant: normal; letter-spacing: normal; font-style: normal; font-weight: normal; orphans: 2; widows: 2">
<span style="display: inline-block; border: none; padding: 0cm"><font face="Arial, sans-serif"><font size="2" style="font-size: 10pt"><font color="#222222">You
should write a program which will find maximum length of&nbsp;<i>The
Golden Age</i>&nbsp;which starts no earlier than the year&nbsp;<font size="3" style="font-size: 12pt"><i>l</i></font>&nbsp;and
ends no later than the year&nbsp;<font size="3" style="font-size: 12pt"><i>r</i></font>.
If all years in the interval&nbsp;<font size="3" style="font-size: 12pt">[</font><font size="3" style="font-size: 12pt"><i>l</i></font><font size="3" style="font-size: 12pt">, </font><font size="3" style="font-size: 12pt"><i>r</i></font><font size="3" style="font-size: 12pt">]</font>&nbsp;are&nbsp;<i>unlucky</i>&nbsp;then
the answer is&nbsp;<font size="2" style="font-size: 11pt">0</font>.</span></font></font></font></p>
