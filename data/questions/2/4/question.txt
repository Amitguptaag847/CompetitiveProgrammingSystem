in a row. You can either turn each rectangle by&nbsp;<font face="MJXc-TeX-main-R, MJXc-TeX-main-Rw">90</font>&nbsp;degrees
or leave it as it is. If you turn a rectangle, its width will be

can not change the order of the rectangles.</b></span></font></font></font></p>
