<p style="margin-bottom: 0cm; border: none; padding: 0cm; font-variant: normal; letter-spacing: normal; font-style: normal; font-weight: normal; orphans: 2; widows: 2">
<span style="display: inline-block; border: none; padding: 0cm"><font face="Arial, sans-serif"><font size="2" style="font-size: 10pt"><font color="#222222">Ravi
loves playing with numbers. He only knows prime numbers that are
digits yet. These numbers are&nbsp;<font size="3" style="font-size: 12pt">2</font>,&nbsp;<font size="3" style="font-size: 12pt">3</font>,&nbsp;<font size="3" style="font-size: 12pt">5</font>&nbsp;and&nbsp;<font size="3" style="font-size: 12pt">7</font>.
But Willy grew rather bored of such numbers, so he came up with a few
games that were connected with them.</span></font></font></font></p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 0cm; border: none; padding: 0cm; font-variant: normal; letter-spacing: normal; font-style: normal; font-weight: normal; orphans: 2; widows: 2">
<span style="display: inline-block; border: none; padding: 0cm"><font face="Arial, sans-serif"><font size="2" style="font-size: 10pt"><font color="#222222">Ravi
wants to find the minimum number of length&nbsp;<font size="3" style="font-size: 12pt"><i>n</i></font>,
such that it is simultaneously divisible by all numbers Willy already
knows (<font size="3" style="font-size: 12pt">2</font>,&nbsp;<font size="3" style="font-size: 12pt">3</font>,&nbsp;<font size="3" style="font-size: 12pt">5</font>&nbsp;and&nbsp;<font size="3" style="font-size: 12pt">7</font>).
Help him with that.</span></font></font></font></p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 0cm; border: none; padding: 0cm; font-variant: normal; letter-spacing: normal; font-style: normal; font-weight: normal; orphans: 2; widows: 2">
<span style="display: inline-block; border: none; padding: 0cm"><font face="Arial, sans-serif"><font size="2" style="font-size: 10pt"><font color="#222222">A
number's length is the number of digits in its decimal representation
without leading zeros.</span></font></font></font></p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 0cm; font-variant: normal; letter-spacing: normal; font-style: normal; orphans: 2; widows: 2">
<span style="display: inline-block; border: none; padding: 0cm"><font face="Arial, sans-serif"><font size="3" style="font-size: 12pt"><b><font color="#222222">Input</span></b></font></font></font></p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 0cm; border: none; padding: 0cm; font-variant: normal; letter-spacing: normal; font-style: normal; font-weight: normal; orphans: 2; widows: 2">
<span style="display: inline-block; border: none; padding: 0cm"><font face="Arial, sans-serif"><font size="2" style="font-size: 10pt"><font color="#222222">A
single input line contains a single integer&nbsp;<font size="3" style="font-size: 12pt"><i>n</i></font>&nbsp;(<font size="3" style="font-size: 12pt">1 ≤ </font><font size="3" style="font-size: 12pt"><i>n</i></font><font size="3" style="font-size: 12pt"> ≤ 10</font><font size="2" style="font-size: 9pt">5</font>).</span></font></font></font></p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 0cm; font-variant: normal; letter-spacing: normal; font-style: normal; orphans: 2; widows: 2">
<span style="display: inline-block; border: none; padding: 0cm"><font face="Arial, sans-serif"><font size="3" style="font-size: 12pt"><b><font color="#222222">Output</span></b></font></font></font></p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 0cm; border: none; padding: 0cm; font-variant: normal; letter-spacing: normal; font-style: normal; font-weight: normal; orphans: 2; widows: 2">
<span style="display: inline-block; border: none; padding: 0cm"><font face="Arial, sans-serif"><font size="2" style="font-size: 10pt"><font color="#222222">Print
a single integer — the answer to the problem without leading
zeroes, or &quot;<font size="3" style="font-size: 11pt">-1</font>&quot;
(without the quotes), if the number that meet the problem condition
does not exist.</span></font></font></font></p>
