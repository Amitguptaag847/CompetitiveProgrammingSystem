<span style="display: inline-block; border: none; padding: 0cm"><font face="Arial, sans-serif"><font size="2" style="font-size: 10pt"><font color="#222222">Today,

<p style="margin-bottom: 0cm; border: none; padding: 0cm; font-variant: normal; letter-spacing: normal; font-style: normal; font-weight: normal; orphans: 2; widows: 2">
<span style="display: inline-block; border: none; padding: 0cm"><font face="Arial, sans-serif"><font size="2" style="font-size: 10pt"><font color="#222222">Amit
is given an array of non-negative integers&nbsp;<font size="3" style="font-size: 12pt"><i>a</i></font><font size="2" style="font-size: 9pt">1</font><font size="3" style="font-size: 12pt">, </font><font size="3" style="font-size: 12pt"><i>a</i></font><font size="2" style="font-size: 9pt">2</font><font size="3" style="font-size: 12pt">, ..., </font><font size="3" style="font-size: 12pt"><i>a</i></font><font size="2" style="font-size: 9pt"><i>n</i></font>.

then he adds&nbsp;<font size="3" style="font-size: 12pt"><i>x</i></font>&nbsp;to
some elements of the array (no more than once), subtract&nbsp;<font size="3" style="font-size: 12pt"><i>x</i></font>&nbsp;from

<p style="margin-bottom: 0cm; border: none; padding: 0cm; font-variant: normal; letter-spacing: normal; font-style: normal; font-weight: normal; orphans: 2; widows: 2">
<span style="display: inline-block; border: none; padding: 0cm"><font face="Arial, sans-serif"><font size="2" style="font-size: 10pt"><font color="#222222">Now

<p style="margin-bottom: 0cm; font-variant: normal; letter-spacing: normal; font-style: normal; orphans: 2; widows: 2">
<span style="display: inline-block; border: none; padding: 0cm"><font face="Arial, sans-serif"><font size="3" style="font-size: 12pt"><b><font color="#222222">Input</span></b></font></font></font></p>

contains&nbsp;<font size="3" style="font-size: 12pt"><i>n</i></font>integers&nbsp;<font size="3" style="font-size: 12pt"><i>a</i></font><font size="2" style="font-size: 9pt">1</font><font size="3" style="font-size: 12pt">, </font><font size="3" style="font-size: 12pt"><i>a</i></font><font size="2" style="font-size: 9pt">2</font><font size="3" style="font-size: 12pt">, ..., </font><font size="3" style="font-size: 12pt"><i>a</i></font><font size="2" style="font-size: 9pt"><i>n</i></font>&nbsp;(<font size="3" style="font-size: 12pt">0 ≤ </font><font size="3" style="font-size: 12pt"><i>a</i></font><font size="2" style="font-size: 9pt"><i>i</i></font><font size="3" style="font-size: 12pt"> ≤ 10</font><sup><font size="2" style="font-size: 9pt">9</font></sup>)&nbsp;—
elements of the array.</span></font></font></font></p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 0cm; font-variant: normal; letter-spacing: normal; font-style: normal; orphans: 2; widows: 2">
<span style="display: inline-block; border: none; padding: 0cm"><font face="Arial, sans-serif"><font size="3" style="font-size: 12pt"><b><font color="#222222">Output</span></b></font></font></font></p>

&quot;<font size="3" style="font-size: 11pt">YES</font>&quot;
